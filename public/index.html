Guideline — Experience Individual Logbook (TVJ-QA-F-057)

No. Description
1 Purpose of this experience individual logbook is for ensuring the maintenance personnel are involved in at least 6 months of actual relevant aircraft or component maintenance experience in any consecutive 2-year period (Refer to TCAR 8 Part 145.A.35 (C) and TCAR PEL Part 66.A.20 (b)(2))
2 This electronic form is based on Experience Individual Logbook (Form No. TVJ-QA-F-057)
3 This form is intended solely for recordkeeping purposes and remains the property of Thai VietJet Air. Unauthorized use, modification, reproduction, or distribution of this form by personnel outside Thai VietJet Air is strictly prohibited. Do not alter any formulas, formatting, or content contained within this electronic form.
4 Certifying Staff Position shall record only Work which you performed Release to Service only
5 Column "Work Ref." need to be filled with Work Order No. as priority. And Technical Log Page as optional
6 Mechanic shall record only Work that you involve only, but cannot select CRS as activity
7 Record only the tasks in which you have been directly involved. All experience records shall be accurate and truthful. Any false or fraudulent entry identified by QA may affect the issuance or renewal of your authorization.
8 Work Ref. for Transit Check shall be record with Flight No.
9 Enter the Work Description "Transit Check, Preflight Check, Daily Check, Weekly Check, APU Change, Engine Change, or A Check". The ATA Chapter, Activity, and Duration will be populated automatically.
10 Work that is CCL Item and rectify within CCL (not transfer to Tech log), do not record as Experience
11 Activity "Supervise" shall be used only when that C/S instruct personnel who are on OJT period
12 1 Working day estimate 8 hours in both Line and Workshop
Example: Workshop working time 0830-1800, 0930 Hr per day but we deduct break time, handover time, preparation and clear area during start and end of the day
Example: Line working time 0830-2030, 1200 Hr per day but we deduct break time, handover time, preparation and clear area during start and end of the day
